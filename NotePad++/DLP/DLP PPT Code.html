Informational Resources / Training
DLP Wiki Page –
DLP Wiki Page is being stood up, and will contain information related to Exchange and
Teams prior to Pilot
Help Desk Training resources - (Under development)
For the DLP Pilot only, CFPB Help desk will not be integrated in responding to Pilot users
questions. A DLP Pilot teams channel will be created for transparent communications from
users to technical team during the Pilot period. However, a pilot user list will be provided to
the helpdesk, and in the event a Pilot users submit a helpdesk ticket, we request the helpdesk
to forward the ticket to the DLP technical Team (Only if they are apart of the Pilot)

Technical Configuration Policy Settings document –Living document, which will be updated

DLP MVP Summary
The purpose, goals and objectives of the DLP MVP for Microsoft Office
Exchange and Teams has proven successful in the Test Tenant.
•DLP policies specific to CUI & PII have been configured and internally tested for
Exchange and Teams in Test
•Users are pointed to information pertaining to why they are receiving a DLP policy
violation
•In Exchange, users must provide a business justification for transmitting sensitive
information outside of the network
•In Teams chat/messaging, users are prevented from transmitting sensitive information
including attachments outside the CFPB network
•Established through API connection DLP log ingestion into Splunk
•Communications and Informational resources / training materials have been developed
•Identified Future DLP iterations
