CFPB End User Guide:

How to Use YubiKey to Authenticate on iOS

Overview

The purpose of this document is to provide authentication instructions when logging into Microsoft apps
on your CFPB issued iOS device, using the 'MS Authenticator' app.

  1.   On the 'Sign In' screen, type your CFPB email address and click 'Next'.

  2.   If you receive the CFPB login screen, do not enter a password, instead click the blue link that says
    ‘Other ways to sign-in’.

     

    Note:  You may not see the above screen if you have used a certificate from PIV-D to
    login before. If prompted with a screen that gives the option to ‘Use a certificate or smart',
    card proceed only if you have a PIV-D ready device. If you proceed and do not have a PIV-D
    installed, this may fail and default back to the screen shown instep 2. If this is happens,
    you can move on to step 3.

  3.   Select 'Sign in with a Security Key'.

  4.  On the ‘Sign In’ screen tap the button next to the 'Security Key' to select it and press 'Continue'.

  5.  When prompted, hold the YubiKey up to the top and back side of the iPhone.

  6.  When prompted, enter the PIN for the YubiKey.

  7.  After entering the PIN, you will be prompted to tap the YubiKey to the top back side of the iPhone.

  8.  If prompted, select your CFPB.gov email address from the list and press ‘Continue’.

    You have successfully used your YubiKey to authenticate to Microsoft 365 apps on your iPhone.
