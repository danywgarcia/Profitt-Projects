CFPB End User Guide:

How to Reset your YubiKey

Overview

Your YubiKey is registered on behalf of end user by the Service Desk using a default PIN. This document is for when an end user does not remember their custom PIN or if the end user has misplaced the YubiKey and will need to reset their YubiKey for security .

For Windows Users

  1. Click the Start menu icon and search for 'Settings', then click on the 'Settings' icon.

  2. Click 'Accounts', then 'Sign-in options'.

  3.   Click ‘Security Key’.

  4. Once expanded, click ‘Manage’.

  5. Touch the metal tab (a gold strip on the side or gold circle) on your YubiKey to verify user presence
    when prompted.

  6. At the next prompt, select ‘Reset Security Key’ to restore the YubiKey to factory settings.

  7. At the warning prompt, click 'Proceed'.

  8. Next, safely remove and re-insert the Yubikey.

  9. Touch the metal tab (a gold strip on the side or gold circle) on your YubiKey twice within 10 seconds
    when prompted.

    Note: If successful, the confirmation message 'Reset Complete' will appear.

For macOS Users

The Yubikey is reset using the YubiKey Manager. If the application is not present, install from Self Service.

  1. Open ‘Yubikey Manager’.

  2. Select 'FIDO2' from the Applications menu.

  3. At the next prompt, click on ‘Reset FIDO’ to restore the YubiKey to factory settings.

  4. At the 'Reset FIDO?' prompt, click ‘Yes’ to confirm resetting the FIDO key.

  5. Next, safely remove and re-insert the Yubikey.

  6. Touch the metal tab (a gold strip on the side or gold circle) on your YubiKey
    when prompted.

    Note: If successful, the confirmation message 'FIDO applications have been reset' will appear.
